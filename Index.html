Favourite Recipe:Naan Bread

This is a very simple and comforting dish to make.

Ingredients
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 1/4 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, salt, and baking powder.
  2. Add warm water, plain yogurt, and olive oil. Knead until smooth.
  3. Cover and let it rest for 1 hour.
  4. Divide dough into balls and roll out into flat circles.
  5. Cook on a hot skillet for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden.
